About us

Khaadi was originally a men’s clothing brand selling basic-colored kurtas. Khaadi immediately questioned the status quo and made a bold statement. Saira Shamoon, brought in her expertise and splashed a vibrant colour palette on every piece, making a simple men’s Kurta an unapologetic fashion statement.

We don’t wait to stagnate. We change, we create the change
