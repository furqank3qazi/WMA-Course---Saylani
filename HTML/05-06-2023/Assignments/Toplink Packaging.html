About us

Toplink Packaging (Pvt) Ltd is a leading importer, manufacturer and supplier of wide range of Adhesive Tapes, with 25 years of experience in the industry. Toplink Packaging serves a range of prestigious companies ranging from Multinationals to Local Clients and Emerging Companies.

Vision

We aspire to be the leader in providing innovative and aesthetically integrated packaging solutions to enable the key business processes of our customers.

Mission

Our mission is to delight our customers by providing innovative packaging products and solutions. Our mission is to be a leader in the products we offer to our Clients.

*Responsibility towards community and Environment
*Ownership and Openness
*Service with Courtesy and Integrity
*Honesty and Commitment towards our Employees and Business Partners
*Attention to Individual Growth, Learning and Development
*Nurturing Continuous Improvement
