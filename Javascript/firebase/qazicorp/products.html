BOPP tape film, also known as Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) tape, is a type of adhesive tape that consists of a polypropylene backing film with an acrylic-based pressure-sensitive adhesive. It is designed to provide a strong bond between surfaces and is commonly used in industrial, packaging, and commercial applications.

Printed Tape helps in branding, traceability and also used as a method of identification. It reduces carton cost and helps to increase business efficiency.

Kraft Paper Adhesive Tape consists of a tape of Kraft paper and a sticky surface applied on one or both sides. The tape is applied by light pressure and has numerous uses, commercially or industrially. Kraft paper tape is produced through the kraft process of obtaining pulp out of wood. Self Adhesive Kraft Tape is ideal for general purpose packaging, shipping, meaning, repairing, reinforced and sealing.
